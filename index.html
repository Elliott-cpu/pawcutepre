About Pawfully Cute

Pawfully Cute is a dog daycare and boarding business located on a family farm in Abbeyknockmoy. We offer a safe, comfortable environment for dogs to socialize, play, and receive basic and advanced training.

Our Services

  • Dog Daycare
  • Dog Boarding
  • Daily Exercise & Outdoor Activities
  • Training: Basic & Advanced
  • Grooming Services

Pricing

Our pricing is designed to accommodate various needs and budgets:

  • Daycare: €30 per day
  • Boarding: €35 per night
  • Training Sessions: From €15 to €20 per hour
